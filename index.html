        About & Help

        This app uses an interactive merge sort. You compare two candidates at a time. Ties keep the left list's order. Keyboard shortcuts: ← Left, T Tie, → Right. Your progress and tier board save automatically in your browser.

        Privacy: no data leaves your device. Share links encode your state in the URL hash only.
